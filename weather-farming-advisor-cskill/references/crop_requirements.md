## Optimal Soil Moisture Range

Most crops grow best with soil moisture between 0.25-0.45 m³/m³:
- Below 0.15: Drought stress
- 0.15-0.25: Dry, monitor closely
- 0.25-0.40: Optimal for most crops
- 0.40-0.50: Moist, good for high-water crops
- Above 0.50: Risk of waterlogging

## Seasonal Considerations

### Cool Season Crops (4-18°C optimal)
- Lettuce, pea, spinach, cabbage, broccoli, onion, garlic
- Plant in early spring or fall
- Tolerate frost

### Warm Season Crops (18-30°C optimal)
- Tomato, corn, pepper, cucumber, bean, squash, melon
- Plant after last frost
- No frost tolerance
